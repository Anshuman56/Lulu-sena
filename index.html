About Us

A Community Rooted
in Gandhian Values

Lulu Sena is a community movement inspired by the timeless teachings of Mahatma Gandhi — truth, non-violence, and selfless service to society.

We believe that real change begins at the grassroots level. Our members come together with a shared commitment to uplift communities, stand for justice, and live by the principles of Satyameva Jayate.

Guided by the spirit of the Indian tricolor, we celebrate unity, peace and the power of collective action for a better tomorrow.
